Digital Experiments: Collections & Geospatial Analysis

A modular record of data linking, mapping, and analytics experiments.

A) Adding coordinates to Places table

Linking Place data to Getty identifiers. This enables the addition of longitude and latitude coordinates to entries, allowing for geospatial analysis of the Place table and linked objects.

Future Work:

Extending linkages to services like NLS and HeritageGateway; creating search apps for geocoding service comparisons.

B & C) Mapping & Museum Counts

Mapping the "place made" field allows for the geospatial analysis of the distribution of object origins. Counts across countries were visualized based on country appearances in the data.
